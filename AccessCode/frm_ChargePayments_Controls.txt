Form: frm_ChargePayments
========================================

Control Name		Type		Caption/Source
------------------------------------------------------------------------
cboInvoice		ComboBox		SELECT dbo_Transactions.TransactionID AS [رقم الفاتورة], dbo_Parties.PartyName AS [العميل/المورد], Sum(dbo_AdditionalCharges.ChargeAmount) AS [إجمالى الرسوم] FROM (dbo_Transactions INNER JOIN dbo_Parties ON dbo_Transactions.PartyID = dbo_Parties.PartyID) INNER JOIN dbo_AdditionalCharges ON dbo_Transactions.TransactionID = dbo_AdditionalCharges.TransactionID GROUP BY dbo_Transactions.TransactionID, dbo_Parties.PartyName; 
Label1		Label		الفاتورة
